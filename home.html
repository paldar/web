Blokus

We have a game called blokus. It has the following features:

It is a strategy game for the whole family. It has 21 pieces per player, of all different shapes. It has a board with raised edges and valleys holds pieces in place. Go out or end up with the least amount of squares left to win! It has includes 84 total pieces, good for four players!
